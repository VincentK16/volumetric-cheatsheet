State tells you where you are. Colour tells you who's winning. But which direction the value is moving tells you what's about to happen.

Even if both oscillators are red and negative — the speed and direction of that change is your earliest warning of what price will do next.

The 3-Question Mental Checklist
1
WHERE is the value? Positive = zone controlled by buyers. Flat = neutral. Negative = sellers in control. This is your baseline.
2
What colour is the line? Green = buyers winning the battle RIGHT NOW. Red = sellers winning RIGHT NOW. Colour changes before value crosses zero — it's your earliest warning.
3
Which direction is it moving? Less negative = momentum shifting, reversal approaching. More negative = acceleration, do not fight it. This is your velocity signal — the earliest of all three layers.
